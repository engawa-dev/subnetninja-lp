Subnet Ninja Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Subnet Ninja is a local-first subnet planning app. We do not collect, sell, track, or share personal data from the app.

Data Collection

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, account registration, or developer-operated backend services.

Local Data

Calculation history, preferences, and Pro unlock state may be stored locally on your device. This data is used only to provide app features and is not sent to us.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy. Subnet Ninja only uses Apple's purchase status to unlock Pro features.

Support

If you contact us for support, we will use the information you provide only to respond to your request.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, please use the Subnet Ninja support page.
